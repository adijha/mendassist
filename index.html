Who Are We

Mendassist is India’s first venture that offers financial help to people who struggle with repairment of their electronic gadgets. We came into service after realising a very common issue that is usually neglected. Majority of Indian people face problems when their gadgets break down. They suffer monetarily when they go to their respective service hubs. People receive easy economic help during their purchase. But, the problem arises when their gadgets cease to work. We realise the trouble majority of Indian families go through. They rely on easy monthly instalments for their basic requirements. So, we decided to incorporate simple financial solutions for the common people. Mendassist provides easy EMI options to the people for fixing up their electronic devices. With our help, you will not have to pay a large sum at once for your service. We are a team of young and highly enthusiast minds who work with the sole aim of client’s satisfaction. Our team works with complete dedication for your betterment. Our partners include all your favourite electronic brands. You can avail our services at your respective service stations. YOU ASK, WE MEND.

Our Vision

We aim to create a hassle-free environment where no individual has to undergo financial extremity in the process of fixing up their electronic gadgets. Our vision also includes expanding our services throughout India so that every Indian can avail our services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide monetary aid to every Indian who battles financially with their impaired devices. We aim to provide impeccable services to our clients. Mendassist also emphasizes on maintaining a work environment in which our employees can flourish professionally

Our Services

Mendassist offers easy monthly instalment options to the people who are not comfortable in paying out the whole sum asked by the service hubs in return for refurbishment or repairment of their electronic gadgets (Mobiles, tablets, laptops, computers). Our customers can avail our services at every authorised service centre of their respective electronic brands. We bestow our services without complications. This means that we do not ask for your credit history or other financial documents for our services. We are partners with every electronic brand that offer their services in India. You can easily get all the details of the payment options at the service hubs. For further clarification, you can contact us through email. Our executives are available round the clock for your service.WE SERVE FOR YOUR EASEMENT.
